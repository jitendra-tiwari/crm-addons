• Dates - {d:n} - The Date inserts the current date at the position of the token in the specified format.
    • Date DD: {dd}, Result: 03
    • Date MM: {MM}, Result: 06
    • Date MMM: {MMM}, Result: 06 Aug.
    • Date YY: {yy}, Result: 16
    • Date YYYY: {yyyy}, Result: 2016
    • Date 0: {0}, Result: 06
